How to Take Proper Images for Disease Detection

1

Take photos in bright, natural daylight for best results

2

Focus on affected areas of the plant, showing both healthy and diseased tissue

3

Include multiple angles of the affected plant part if possible

4

Ensure the image is in focus and not blurry

5

Avoid shadows or reflections on the plant surface

About AgriCure

Our Mission

To empower farmers worldwide with accessible technology for crop disease detection and treatment, improving agricultural productivity and sustainability.

Who We Are

AgriCure is an advanced agricultural technology platform that helps farmers quickly identify crop diseases and get instant medicine recommendations. Our AI-powered system analyzes crop photos to detect diseases with high accuracy, providing farmers with actionable insights to protect their crops.

Our Technology

We use state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of crop disease images. Our system can identify over 100 different crop diseases with 97% accuracy, making it one of the most reliable tools for farmers.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we've helped over 50,000 farmers worldwide detect and treat crop diseases, saving millions of dollars in potential crop losses and reducing the use of unnecessary pesticides by up to 30%.
